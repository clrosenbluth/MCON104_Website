Pod Learning

Education pods have risen in popularity during the COVID-19 pandemic, but their benefits go far beyond social distancing. Students in pods have the opportunity to practice social skills while making the most of a low student-to-teacher ratio. This method also gives parents greater control while leaving time for parents to work outside the home. Learn more about pod learning here.
