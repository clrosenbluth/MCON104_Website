Pricing

We are dedicated to providing you with the education you need, at the price you need. Our pricing is flexible and individualized, based on your unique mixture of education methods.

Here's a list of typical price ranges for each of our methods. If you're looking for a more accurate estimate, please feel free to contact us or fill out the form below.

  • Homeschooling: $100-$350 per month (depending on the chosen curriculum and grade level)
  • Pod Learning: $800-$1100 per month (this price can be broken down between families)
  • Hybrid Learning: $300-$700 per month (depending on the balance of methods chosen)
  • Tutoring: $40-$75 per session
